Sweet Chili Soy Sauce

Description

This sweet chili soy sauce is great for drizzling on roasted vegetables, such as bok choy, green beans, and eggplant.

Ingredients

  • Light soy sauce (reduced sodium)
  • Brown sugar
  • Mae Ploy Sweet Chili Sauce

Steps

  1. Add 1 part soy sauce to 1 part brown sugar to a sauce pan, reduce over medium/low heat, careful to not let it boil over and burn the brown sugar. Reduce until thick. Transfer sweet soy sauce to a glass container for storage.
  2. Mix sweet soy sauce with the sweet chili sauce to taste (roughly 1:4 ratio).
  3. Drizzle to taste over roasted vegetables.
